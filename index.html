Welcome For Avanticulam Education

The society aims to provide free education to underprivileged students, helping them prepare for board exams NTSE, OLYMPIAD, JEE, AND NEET.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, Avantikulam began by offering online classes via Zoom to students from Kendriya Vidyalayas and Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas, reaching over 2,500 students. The initiative has since expanded to include a dedicated website and Android application to further support students.

This is how we teach those students and also we have a mentorship program to help them.

The project has been acknowledged by MANIT's Professors (Savita Dixit) as well as various school principals.
